## Overview

After signing in, you are taken to the dashboard at `modules/dashboard.php`. This is the home screen of the system.

The **left sidebar** displays:
- The **SIS-BIBLIO** header (book icon + system name)
- Your **full name** and **role level** (ADMINISTRADOR or LECTOR REGULAR) beneath the header
- Navigation links to all available modules

---

## Statistics for administrators

When you are signed in as an **Administrator (Rol 1)**, the dashboard shows three stat cards:

| Card | Source |
|------|--------|
| **Total Libros Catálogo** | COUNT of all rows in the `libros` table |
| **Lectores Registrados** | COUNT of all rows in the `lectores` table |
| **Préstamos Activos (Global)** | COUNT of rows in `prestamos` where status is not `Cancelada` |

---

## Statistics for readers

When you are signed in as a **Reader (Rol 2)**, the dashboard shows two stat cards:

| Card | Source |
|------|--------|
| **Total Libros Catálogo** | COUNT of all rows in the `libros` table |
| **Mis Préstamos Históricos** | COUNT of loans linked to your own user account |

<Note>
  Readers do not see the global active loan count or the registered readers count. Those stat cards are hidden and replaced with your personal loan history.
</Note>

---

## Navigation

The left sidebar provides links to all modules you have access to:

- **Inicio** — Returns to this home screen (`dashboard.php`)
- **Catálogo** — Book catalog (`modules/libros.php`)
- **Mis Préstamos** — Loan management (`modules/prestamos.php`)
- **Lectores** — Reader directory (`modules/lectores.php`) — *Administrators only*
- **Staff del Sistema** — User management (`modules/usuarios.php`) — *Administrators only*
- **Salir** — Signs you out and redirects to `login.php`

<Info>
  Readers will not see the **Lectores** or **Staff del Sistema** links in their sidebar. Attempting to access those pages directly will result in an "Acceso Denegado" error.
</Info>

---

## Mobile navigation

On small screens (smartphones and tablets), the sidebar is hidden by default to save screen space.

Tap the **hamburger menu (☰)** in the top-left corner to expand the sidebar and access the navigation links.
