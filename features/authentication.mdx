## Signing in

Navigate to `login.php` to access the system. Enter your **email address** and **password**, then click **Ingresar**.

<Tip>
  Click the eye icon (👁) next to the password field to reveal what you have typed before submitting. This helps you catch typos before the form is submitted.
</Tip>

A **Volver al Inicio** button appears in the top-left corner of the login page. Click it to return to the landing page without signing in.

On a successful login, you are redirected to `modules/dashboard.php`.

---

## Account lockout

After **3 consecutive failed login attempts**, your account is temporarily locked.

<Warning>
  If you enter the wrong password 3 times in a row, your account is locked for **15 minutes**. You will see the message: *"Demasiados intentos. Cuenta bloqueada por 15 minutos."* Wait 15 minutes and try again — the lock clears automatically. No admin action is required.
</Warning>

## Registering as a reader

New accounts can be created from the public registration page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the registration page">
    On the login page, click **Regístrate aquí** at the bottom, or navigate directly to `register.php`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in your details">
    Enter your **Full Name**, **email address**, and a **password** (minimum 5 characters). The password field also has an eye icon to verify what you are typing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Registrarse y Entrar**. The system creates your account and immediately signs you in, redirecting you to the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  All accounts created through the public registration page are assigned the **Reader** role automatically. A reader profile (library card) is also created for you at the same time, linked to your new account.
</Note>

## User roles

SIS-BIBLIO has two roles. Your role determines which modules and actions are available to you.

| | **Administrator (Rol 1)** | **Reader (Rol 2)** |
|---|---|---|
| **Book catalog** | View, add, edit, delete | View only |
| **Loans** | View all loans; add, edit, delete, cancel | View own loans; create and cancel own loans |
| **Readers** | Full management | Not accessible |
| **Staff users** | Full management | Not accessible |
| **Dashboard** | Total books, registered readers, global active loans | Total books, own historical loan count |

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Administrator" icon="shield">
    Full CRUD access to books, loans, readers, and staff accounts. Sees global statistics on the dashboard.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reader" icon="book-open">
    Can view the book catalog and their own loans. Sees only their own loan count on the dashboard.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Signing out

Click the **Salir** button in the left sidebar to end your session. This destroys your session data on the server.

<Note>
  Always use the **Salir** button rather than just closing the browser tab. Closing the tab leaves your session active on the server, which is a security risk on shared computers.
</Note>
