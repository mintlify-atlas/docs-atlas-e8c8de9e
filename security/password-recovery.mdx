If you forget your password, SIS-BIBLIO can send a secure reset link to your registered email address. The link expires after **1 hour** and can only be used once — after it is used or expires, it becomes invalid.

## Requesting a reset

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the recovery page">
    On the login page, click **Olvidé mi contraseña** below the sign-in form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your registered email address">
    Type the email address associated with your account and click **Enviar Enlace a Mi Correo**.

    <Note>
      For security, the system always shows a confirmation message regardless of whether the email address exists in the database. This prevents account enumeration.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your inbox">
    Open your email inbox and look for a message from the library system with the subject **Recuperación de Contraseña SIS-BIBLIOTECA**.

    <Tip>
      If the email doesn't arrive within a few minutes, check your **spam** or **junk** folder — automated messages are sometimes filtered there.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the reset link">
    Click the **Restablecer Contraseña** button in the email. This opens the password reset form (`reset.php`) in your browser.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Setting a new password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter your new password">
    On the reset page (`reset.php`), type your new password into the field. The password must be at least 5 characters long.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify what you typed">
    Click the eye icon next to the password field to reveal the text and confirm you typed what you intended.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Click **Actualizar y Entrar** to save your new password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with your new credentials">
    You are redirected to the login page. Enter your email address and the new password you just set.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Token behavior

<Note>
  The reset link contains a unique, single-use token stored in the `reset_token` column of the `usuarios` table, with an expiry timestamp in `reset_expires`.

  - Once the link is used to set a new password, the token is cleared from the database and cannot be reused.
  - If you open the link after the 1-hour window, the system will reject it with an expiry message.
  - If your link expires before you use it, return to the login page and click **Olvidé mi contraseña** to request a new one.
</Note>

## Email configuration requirement

<Warning>
  Password recovery depends on PHPMailer being configured with a valid SMTP account (Gmail or equivalent). If your administrator has not set up SMTP credentials, the recovery email will not be sent and you will see an error on the recovery page.

  Contact your system administrator if recovery emails are not arriving. For setup instructions, see [Installation](/installation).
</Warning>
