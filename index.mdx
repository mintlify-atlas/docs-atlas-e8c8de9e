## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install and configure">
    Copy the project to your web server, import the SQL schema, and set your database credentials in `config/database.php`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Open `index.php` in your browser and click **Acceder al Panel**. Use the pre-seeded admin account or register a new reader account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage your library">
    Add books to the catalog, register readers, and record loans with due dates — all from the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay secure">
    Every action is protected by CSRF tokens, prepared statements, and role-based access control. Passwords are hashed with BCrypt and accounts lock after repeated failed attempts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  SIS-BIBLIO ships with two pre-seeded test accounts: an **Administrator** (`admin@ejemplo.com`) and a **Reader** (`lector@ejemplo.com`), both using password `1234567`. Change these before deploying to production.
</Note>
